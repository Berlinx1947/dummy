Our 6D Process

Structured workflow ensuring accurate, efficient, and compliant medical billing for every client

01

Data Collection

The initial phase involves acquiring comprehensive patient demographic and insurance information, which is crucial for accurate billing and claim submission.

02

Diagnosis Entry

The medical diagnoses are carefully recorded using the appropriate ICD codes, essential for insurance companies to determine coverage and payment.

03

Detailing Services

This involves itemizing all procedures and services provided to the patient, ensuring that each is documented and coded correctly for billing purposes.

04

Dispatching Claims

Claims are meticulously prepared and submitted to insurance companies for processing, preventing errors that could lead to denials or delays.

05

Distribution of Funds

Payments received from insurers and patients are posted to their respective accounts, with careful tracking to ensure accurate financial records.

06

Delinquency Resolution

This final phase addresses any outstanding claims and patient balances, including follow-ups on unpaid claims, denial management, and ensuring all services are compensated.
